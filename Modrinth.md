Add-on for pajic's Accessorify made by GK1H.
<h1>Features</h1>
<h2>Both Fabric & NeoForge</h2>
Compat for putting the Totem of Neverdying from <a href="https://modrinth.com/mod/netherite-extras">Netherite Extras</a> in the charm/totem slot.
<h2>NeoForge</h2>
Compat for putting <a href="https://modrinth.com/mod/reinforced-shulker-boxes">Reinforced Shulker Boxes</a> in the shulker slot. (Requires <a href="https://modrinth.com/mod/gk1h-tags">GK1H Tags</a>.)
